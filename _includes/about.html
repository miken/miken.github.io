About Me

Hailing from the tropical paradise of Vietnam, I grew up memorizing math equations, tinkering with computer parts, feasting on rambutans and coconuts, and attempting stints in choir, marching band, and dance company.

In search of my true passion, I set foot on temperate New England, emerged with a degree in Economics from Williams College four years later, and found a calling in product management and software development.

In my day time, I manage data analytics products for a nonprofit that shares stakeholders perspectives with their foundation clients and help foundations make better decision through advisory and research work.

In my spare time, I enjoy developing Web sites using various frameworks, from Ruby on Rails, Django, to meteor.

In my remaining spare time, I like cooking and rowing.
