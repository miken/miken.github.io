About Me

I have fond memories of my childhood in tropical Vietnam. I spent much time solving trigonometry equations, tinkering with computers, and feasting on rambutans.

Since leaving home at the age of fourteen, I have lived in Singapore, Poland, and the U.S. I graduated from Williams College with a degree in Economics. I also graduated from breaking computer hardware to writing software. I speak several languages and program in several others.

During day time, I manage data analytics products for a nonprofit that helps foundations make better strategic decisions.

During spare time, I develop Web sites using various frameworks, from Ruby on Rails, Django, to meteor.

During my remaining spare time, I cook and row. I make some good pho and paella. I like the swooshing sound of an oar blade cleanly traversing water.

I enjoy building great technology products for people and organizations. If you have an interesting idea, share it with me.
